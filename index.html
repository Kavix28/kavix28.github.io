About Us

DHAWAN AND ASSOCIATES

Dhawan & Associates is a distinguished law firm based in Delhi/NCR, committed to delivering comprehensive legal and litigation services with a client-centric approach. Founded by Advocate Anjali Dhawan, who has been practicing in the Delhi High Court since 2011, and Advocate Yatin Dhawan, who has been practicing since 2021, our firm upholds a tradition of excellence, integrity, and dedication to justice. With a team of highly skilled advocates, solicitors, and legal consultants, we specialize in providing expert legal solutions across various practice areas. Our focus is on understanding the unique needs of our clients and delivering tailored legal strategies that ensure the best possible outcomes. At Dhawan & Associates, we take pride in our commitment to professionalism, legal expertise, and a results-driven approach that sets us apart in the legal fraternity. Our clientele includes individuals, businesses, and organizations, both nationally and internationally, who trust us for our unwavering dedication and legal acumen.

ANJALI DHAWAN

Advocate Anjali Dhawan is a highly esteemed legal professional with over a decade of experience in the Delhi High Court. Practicing since 2011, she has built a strong reputation for her in-depth legal expertise, strategic thinking, and client-focused approach. Her proficiency spans multiple areas of law, including civil, corporate, and criminal litigation, ensuring comprehensive legal solutions for her clients. With a commitment to justice and an unwavering dedication to her clients, Advocate Anjali Dhawan has successfully represented individuals and businesses in complex legal matters. Her meticulous attention to detail, coupled with her ability to navigate challenging legal landscapes, has made her a trusted name in the legal fraternity.

YATIN DHAWAN

Advocate Yatin Dhawan is a dynamic and result-oriented legal professional, practicing in the Delhi High Court since 2021. As a founding member of Dhawan & Associates, he brings fresh perspectives, modern legal strategies, and a strong analytical approach to every case he handles. His expertise covers various domains of law, including corporate disputes, civil litigation, and legal advisory services. With a strong commitment to upholding justice, Advocate Yatin Dhawan is dedicated to providing effective legal representation and tailored solutions for his clients. His passion for law and sharp problem-solving skills make him a valuable asset to the firm and a trusted advocate for those seeking legal guidance.
